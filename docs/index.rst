MQTT IO: Expose GPIO, sensors and more over MQTT
================================================

**MQTT IO** enables control over hardware devices using messages published to MQTT topics.

GPIO inputs, sensors and serial streams can be published to MQTT when they change, allowing you to integrate your devices into larger systems including but not limited to `Home Assistant <https://www.home-assistant.io/>`_.
